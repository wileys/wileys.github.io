Close the gap.

The gender wage gap is a serious issue that must be surpassed for the true equality of different identities.

Learn more

Play our game. A short, educational platformer.

We created Close the Gap as our final project for our Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Sephora. Each level, and the gaps between the platforms within them, simulate the difficulty of identifying as a woman in the workforce. Usually, the wage gap differs with race, much like how our game's levels become more challenging as the wage gap widens. We built this short, satyrical game to spread awareness of the gender wage gap, for it is a serious matter that everyone must acknowledge and unify to change.
